YOGA DURING THE PANDEMIC

To live through this lockdown & pandemic period, yoga is the best thing to adopt as a lifestyle habit. It helps us build a strong physical, mental and spiritual health system. When combined with breathing and meditation, it acts as the best element to take care of our mind, body and soul.



Ustrasana Strengthen and tone abdominal muscles.

Utthita Hasta Padangushtasana Open up back of your knees to relieve leg cramps.

Parsvakonasana Strengthen the spine, hamstrings

Ardha Matsyendrasana Spark up your digestion

Gomukhasana Chest and shoulder opener

Paripurna Navasana Tones your abdominal, lower back muscles
